Welcome to the homepage for the MIT Sport Taekwondo Club! We are dedicated to providing a place for taekwondoists and aspiring martial artists of all abilities (from absolute beginners to high-ranking black belts) to train together and improve their skills, all while becoming part of an exciting and close-knit community. The club has become a powerhouse in the league in recent years, with multiple ECTC league and national championship titles to its name. Come join us for a workout, or email sport-tkd-info@mit.edu, and find out what all the fuss is about!
