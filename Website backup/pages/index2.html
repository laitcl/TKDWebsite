Welcome to the homepage for the MIT Sport Taekwondo Club! We are dedicated to providing a place for taekwondoists and aspiring martial artists of all abilities (from absolute beginners to high-ranking black belts) to train together and improve their skills, all while becoming part of an exciting and close-knit community. The club has become a powerhouse in the league in recent years, with multiple ECTC league and national championship titles to its name. Come join us for a workout, or email sport-tkd-info@mit.edu, and find out what all the fuss is about!

05.27.2012 || MIT alum at World University Taekwondo Championships

This past weekend, MIT Sport Taekwondo alum Rene Chen competed in the World University Taekwondo Championships in Pocheon, Korea, where she placed 7th in both women's individual and women's team poomsae as part of the US National Collegiate Taekwondo Team. This is the second consecutive year that Rene has made the finals, and we are very lucky to have her as an active member of our community. Congratulations Rene!

01.13.2012 || MIT to host 37th National Collegiate Taekwondo Championships

The Massachusetts Institute of Technology Sport Taekwondo Club is proud to host the 37th National Collegiate Taekwondo Championships on Saturday and Sunday, April 7-8, 2012. These championships also serve as the team trials for student athletes who are interested in earning a spot on the 2012 US National Collegiate Taekwondo Team, which will compete at the 12th World University Taekwondo Championships in Pocheon, Korea, from May 25-30, 2012. The 37th National Collegiate Taekwondo Championships will include forms and sparring competition for both color and black belts.

More information can be found on the nationals website
