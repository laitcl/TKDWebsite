Executive Board

Lawrence Lai
President
Lawrence Lai is a sixth year PhD candidate in the Department of Chemical Engineering. Lawrence joined in February of 2016 as a White Belt, and promoted as a first Dan in May 2018. Raised in Hong Kong's twisted culture, Lawrence is an achievement oriented individual, accomplishing great heights in a large variety of skills including Ballet (Royal Academy of Dance Grade 8), Piano (ABRSM Grade 8), Taiko no Tatsujin (5th Dan), Badminton (Hong Kong Bronze Grade), League of Legends (Season 7 Diamond), and Hearthstone (GVG Legend). As the President of MIT Sport Taekwondo 2018-2019, Lawrence hopes to solve problems through unconventional means, putting his unorthodox mind at work.
 
Gina Yuan
Vice President of External Relations
Gina Yuan is a senior in Course 6 from the California East Bay. She joined taekwondo through the PE class her freshman year and has since evolved from noob to black stripe. Through the club, she has found wonderful friends, teammates to look up to, and a tight community at MIT. Outside of taekwondo, Gina also enjoys running, snowboarding, and baking bread.
