The success of the MIT Sport Taekwondo program draws from a motivated member base, a dedicated coaching staff, and an extensive alumni network. Many alumni now serve as instructors; others in the area still come regularly for practice; still others come back each year for the team's annual Alumni Weekend, usually held the weekend of the MIT Tournament.
