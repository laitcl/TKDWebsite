Social Events

Our team views the social aspect of the team as an integral part of the club's values. This is not only a team for improvement in Taekwondo, but also a home to many MIT students that choose to spend time with each other. The tight knit culture of our team is an important factor that leads to our success.



Our team hosts social events such as barbeques, cabin trips, social gatherings, study session, movie nights, joins 5k runs, and more.

Please check out our Facebook page for more information on social events.
