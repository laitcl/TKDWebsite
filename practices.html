Open workout is held in the Dupont Basketball Court and is led by the club assistant instructors. Practice Description

Regular Workout

Regular workouts meet in Dupont Basketball Court. Everyone is welcome to attend. These classes are almost always led by Master Chuang and taught by all of our instructors. They cover everything from basic techniques to sparring drills and are very well attended.

Sparring Workout

Sparring workout is held on Friday in Dupont Basketball Court. The class focuses on Olympic-style sparring techniques. The class is open to all students who are yellow belt or higher and they should remember to bring all of their sparring gear.
